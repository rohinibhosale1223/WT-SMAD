About Brackeys Game Jam

Brackeys Game Jam is an exciting game development event organized by the popular YouTube channel "Brackeys." The jam brings together game developers of all skill levels to create games based on a specific theme within a limited time frame.

Format

The Brackeys Game Jam typically lasts for a set duration, such as a weekend or several days. Participants are given a theme or prompt at the beginning of the jam, which serves as the basis for their game designs. The event encourages creativity, collaboration, and experimentation.

Location

Brackeys Game Jam is primarily an online event, allowing participants from around the world to join remotely from their homes or studios. However, some participants may choose to gather at physical locations for collaboration and camaraderie.

Theme

Each iteration of the Brackeys Game Jam features a unique theme chosen by the organizers. The theme serves as a creative constraint for participants, inspiring them to explore new ideas and approaches to game development.

Community

Brackeys Game Jam has a vibrant and supportive community of game developers who participate in the event each year. Participants share their progress, exchange feedback, and play each other's games, fostering collaboration and learning.
