About Ludum Dare

Ludum Dare is a popular game jam event known for its strong focus on solo game development and its emphasis on rapid prototyping and iteration. It provides a platform for game developers of all skill levels to challenge themselves, experiment with new ideas, and connect with a vibrant community of fellow developers.

Format

Ludum Dare typically occurs over a weekend, starting on a Friday and ending on a Sunday. Participants have 48 hours to create a game from scratch based on a theme announced at the beginning of the event. There are also "Compo" and "Jam" categories, with the Compo category being more strict (solo work, limited tools/resources) and the Jam category being more relaxed (teams allowed, more time).

Theme

Each Ludum Dare event has a theme that serves as a prompt for participants to base their games on. The theme is announced at the start of the event, and participants are encouraged to interpret it creatively in their game designs.

Location

Ludum Dare is an online event, allowing participants from around the world to join remotely from the comfort of their homes.

Community

Ludum Dare has a strong and active community of game developers who participate in the event regularly. Participants often share their progress, exchange feedback, and play each other's games during and after the event.

History

Ludum Dare has been running since 2002 and has grown into one of the largest and most well-known game jam events in the world. It has inspired countless developers to create innovative and creative games under tight time constraints.
