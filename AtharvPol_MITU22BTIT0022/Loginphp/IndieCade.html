About IndieCade

IndieCade is an annual international festival of independent games. It celebrates independent game development and provides a platform for showcasing innovative and creative games from indie developers around the world.

Format

The IndieCade Game Jam typically takes place over a weekend or several days, during which participants are tasked with creating games based on specific themes or prompts. The event encourages collaboration, experimentation, and the exploration of new ideas in game development.

Location

IndieCade Game Jams may take place at various locations, including physical venues such as game studios, universities, or community centers, as well as online platforms.

Theme

Each IndieCade Game Jam may have a specific theme or prompt that participants are encouraged to incorporate into their game designs. Themes are often announced at the beginning of the jam, sparking creativity and innovation among participants.

Community

IndieCade Game Jams attract a diverse and inclusive community of game developers, artists, designers, and enthusiasts. Participants have the opportunity to network, collaborate, and learn from one another while creating games in a supportive environment.

Website

For more information about IndieCade, including upcoming events, registration details, and past game jam themes, visit the official website: IndieCade.
