About Global Game Jam

The Global Game Jam (GGJ) is the world's largest game jam event. It brings together thousands of game developers from across the globe to create games in a short time span. The event typically takes place over a weekend, during which participants work in teams to develop games based on a common theme.

Founded in 2009, GGJ has grown rapidly, with hundreds of locations worldwide hosting jam sites each year. It aims to foster creativity, collaboration, and experimentation in game development.

Registration

To participate in the Global Game Jam, you can register through the official website. Registration typically opens a few weeks before the event. It's important to register early to secure your spot and receive important updates and resources.

Registration is open to anyone interested in game development, regardless of skill level or experience. You can join as an individual or form a team with friends or fellow jammers at the jam site.

Tips for Participants

  • Come prepared with your development tools and equipment.
  • Be open to collaborating with others and trying new ideas.
  • Stay focused and manage your time effectively during the jam.
  • Take breaks and stay hydrated to maintain energy levels.
  • Have fun and enjoy the creative process!
